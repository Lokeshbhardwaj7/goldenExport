Talc Powder


Talc is also known as Soapstone or Magnesium Silicate. This is a natural mineral and it has uses in different industries such as Rubber Industries, Paint Industries, Food Industries, Paper Industries. Golden Exports is a Exporters and Suppliers of Talc Powder in India. Talcum powder which is also known as Talc is used by women and men to reanimate or fresh up with good perfume. Talcum powder is made from talc, a mineral made up mainly of the elements magnesium, silicon, and oxygen. As a powder, it absorbs moisture well and helps cut down on friction, making it useful for keeping skin dry and helping to prevent rashes. Talcum powder helps in preventing irritation from rashes to the skin and gives freshness. Manufacturers crush, dry and mill talc into a fine, soft, white powder called talcum powder. The powder serves as a lubricant and adds softness and shine to products. The talc is known as "soapstone" is also widely used as a filler or lubricant. This soft rock is easily carved and has been used to make ornamental and practical objects for thousands of years. It has been used to make sculptures, bowls, countertops, sinks, hearths, pipe bowls and also used for Paints grade, cosmetic many other objects.
